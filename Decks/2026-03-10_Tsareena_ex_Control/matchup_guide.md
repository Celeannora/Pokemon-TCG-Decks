# Tsareena ex Control — Matchup Guide (Standard 2026)

---

## How to use this guide

For each matchup: identify their win condition, what you need to lock with Icicle Sole, and whether Toedscruel's Slime Mold Colony is critical. Icicle Sole targets their key attacker. Boss's Orders pulls their setup Pokémon to stall.

---

## Charizard ex / Pidgeot ex

**Estimated win rate**: 60%

**Their win condition**: Get Pidgeot ex up for Quick Search engine; swing with Charizard ex for 180+ damage.

**Key threats**:
- Pidgeot ex Quick Search engine gives consistent access to any card
- Charizard ex 330 HP is hard to knock out without Trop Kick + prior Icicle Sole

**Boss's Orders targets**: Charmander on bench (before it evolves)

**Game plan**:
- *Early*: Use Buddy-Buddy Poffin for Bounsweet + Toedscool. Get Toedscruel up T2-T3 to prevent them from recovering discarded resources
- *Mid*: Icicle Sole Charizard ex to 30 HP. Iono to disrupt their hand. Use Arven to find Rescue Board
- *Late*: Trop Kick the crippled Charizard ex. Counter Catcher or Boss's Orders to gust another target

**Critical turn**: Turn 3 — Toedscruel must be live before they get Pidgeot ex online, or Quick Search refills their hand around Iono disruption

**Cards that win this matchup**: Toedscruel, Iono, Boss's Orders
**Cards that lose this matchup**: If Tsareena ex is Metal-hit (rare in this matchup — no Metal Pokémon used)

## Dragapult ex

**Estimated win rate**: 55%

**Their win condition**: Phantom Dive spreads 6 damage counters across your bench; Fantasy Flight for 200 to Active.

**Key threats**:
- Bench spread kills your Bounsweet/Toedscool before they evolve
- 320 HP Dragapult ex is hard to 2HKO

**Boss's Orders targets**: Dreepy on bench before T2 Dragapult ex

**Game plan**:
- *Early*: Play Shaymin DRI to Active — Flower Curtain protects all non-Rule-Box benched Pokémon from attack effects. This BLOCKS Phantom Dive damage counters from landing on your basics!
- *Mid*: Icicle Sole Dragapult ex to 30 HP. Trop Kick for the KO
- *Late*: Toedscruel prevents Bravery Charm / Super Rod recovery loops

**Critical turn**: Turn 1 — Shaymin must be on bench immediately to prevent early spread wiping your setup

**Cards that win this matchup**: Shaymin, Toedscruel, Tsareena ex
**Cards that lose this matchup**: Losing Shaymin before it provides protection

## Gardevoir ex

**Estimated win rate**: 55%

**Their win condition**: Psychic Embrace accelerates Energy; Gardevoir ex attacks for massive damage scaled to energy in play.

**Key threats**:
- Gardevoir ex 310 HP matches Tsareena ex
- Psychic weakness on Stage 1s like Toedscruel (if they use Psychic attackers)

**Boss's Orders targets**: Kirlia on bench — pulling Kirlia before it evolves to Gardevoir ex delays their setup by 1-2 turns

**Game plan**:
- *Early*: Toedscruel is your MVP — blocks Gardenia's Vigor and other discard recovery Supporters they might include
- *Mid*: Icicle Sole Gardevoir ex. Iono when they have a full hand from Refinement
- *Late*: Arven to find Rescue Board so Tsareena ex can freely rotate

**Critical turn**: Turn 3 — must have Toedscruel live before they start Psychic Embrace loops

**Cards that win this matchup**: Toedscruel, Iono
**Cards that lose this matchup**: Psychic weakness on Toedscruel if opponent runs Gardevoir as attacker

## Raging Bolt ex

**Estimated win rate**: 40%

**Their win condition**: Thunderous Rumble scales damage based on Energy on their side; can one-shot 310 HP Pokémon with setup.

**Key threats**:
- Fastest meta attacker — can hit T2 OHKO potential on Tsareena ex
- Grass weakness means Tsareena ex hits their Pokémon for ×2... but Raging Bolt ex is Lightning, not Grass

**Boss's Orders targets**: Bench Pokémon that has Energy attached (deprive Thunderous Rumble damage scaling)

**Game plan**:
- *Early*: Do NOT rush Tsareena ex Active until you have 2+ energy. Use Comfey and Shaymin as disposable starters
- *Mid*: Icicle Sole Raging Bolt ex to 30 HP. If they heal, Iono to deny healing items
- *Late*: Toedscruel locks out healing recovery. Unfair Stamp when behind on prizes to reset their hand

**Critical turn**: Turn 2 — if they get Thunderous Rumble online before you have Tsareena ex, you're immediately on the back foot

**Cards that win this matchup**: Toedscruel, Unfair Stamp, Iono at 1 prize
**Cards that lose this matchup**: Slow Tsareena ex setup

## Iron Hands ex / Stall variants

**Estimated win rate**: 65%

**Their win condition**: Flip Flop (Iron Hands ex) takes extra prizes when ahead; stall decks try to deck you out.

**Key threats**:
- Iron Hands ex gets extra prize turns if they bank energy
- Stall decks run massive healing recursion (exactly what Toedscruel counters)

**Boss's Orders targets**: Iron Hands ex directly — pull to Active and Icicle Sole it

**Game plan**:
- *Early*: Get Toedscruel on bench immediately — this single card dismantles stall strategies relying on discard recovery
- *Mid*: Icicle Sole every attacker they present. They can't heal through Toedscruel + Iono loops
- *Late*: Briar when they're at 2 prizes for extra prize swing

**Critical turn**: Turn 2 — Toedscruel live = they lose most of their engine

**Cards that win this matchup**: Toedscruel (essential), Briar (closer)
**Cards that lose this matchup**: Nothing specifically — this is a favorable matchup

## Teal Mask Ogerpon ex

**Estimated win rate**: 45%

**Their win condition**: Teal Dance accelerates Grass Energy; Myriad Leaf Shower scales to total energy and goes through Weakness

**Key threats**:
- Tsareena ex is Metal-weak; Ogerpon ex is Grass — no weakness advantage
- Teal Dance ignores normal energy attachment limits
- This is a pure speed race — they're faster

**Boss's Orders targets**: Supporting Pokémon on bench (Ogerpon ex has 210 HP, hard to prep)

**Game plan**:
- *Early*: Prioritize Tsareena ex setup AND keep a second one on bench (Tera bench safety)
- *Mid*: Icicle Sole Ogerpon ex. They will try to Trop Kick heal — Toedscruel prevents Special Condition cure recursion through discard
- *Late*: Unfair Stamp when behind. Counter Catcher when prize count favors

**Critical turn**: Turn 3 — Icicle Sole must hit their Ogerpon ex before they accumulate 5+ energy for a huge Myriad Leaf Shower

**Cards that win this matchup**: Toedscruel lock, Iono disruption, Unfair Stamp
**Cards that lose this matchup**: Tsareena ex taking Metal hits; energy deficit
